How to sign in to Co-Trip

Using Your Co-Trip account

To sign in to Co-Trip, you must have an Co-Trip account. If you used Co-Trip after the 1th of Nov 2012, then you have such an account. If you do not have one, click this link to create one.

If you have an Co-Trip account but forgot your user name or password, click this link to retrieve them.

Sign-in problems

If clicking on Co-Trip links repeatedly brings you to the sign-in page please read the following.

  1. To use Co-Trip you must enable cookies on your browser. Please check that you have cookies enabled.
  2. Co-Trip will automatically terminate your session after two hours of inactivity, so it is normal that you are asked to sign in again if you did not work with it for more than two hours. Another possible reason for this problem is the use of two different browsers or two different computers to access your Co-Trip account. For security reasons, Co-Trip does not allow for a simultaneous access of the same account from more than one browser or computer.

If you have any other problems which prevent you from signing in, please get in touch with the organizers of the relevant conference.
